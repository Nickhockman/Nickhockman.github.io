Hello I am Nick Hockman, a student from SJSU. The work in this portfolio was directly from the projects I worked on for Intro Digital Video. As an artist, I care about improving people's faults when it comes to distractions or addictions. I work on trying to improve myself and through projects and videos I hope for improving others. In my work I go through problems this generation aregoing through such as short attention span and digital distractions. Other works I produce falls under comedy. It's a nice break from all the seriousness of the work and something I genuinely enjoy creating.

My projects help me realize my potential in making videos. For the first two videos I produced were about me and my problems and how the first step to change is to accept you have a problem. Over time I wanted to make more videos that may interest me more in comedy since I've enjoyed making funny videos.
